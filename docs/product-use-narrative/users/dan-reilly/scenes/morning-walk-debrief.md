# Morning walk debrief

**Voice:** persona
**Temporal:** mornings, after the walk
**Journey:** a1c
**Personas:** coach

## Briefing

Came back from the morning walk. The body knows something the rest of me is still arguing with: the A1C number, the BMI, the family history I keep trying to outsource to "later."

This isn't where I want a meal plan or a workout template. It's where I think out loud about what I noticed today — what I ate yesterday, how I slept, whether the walk felt like obligation or like the body asking for it. Whether I'm taking the doctor's six-month deadline seriously or treating it as background noise the way I did the last two physicals.

The mirror helps me hear the difference between "I'm doing the work" (which I can recite) and "the number will move" (which is the only thing that matters in October).
