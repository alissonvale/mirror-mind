**Voice:** persona
**Temporal:** Friday mornings
**Journey:** the-essays
**Personas:** essayist
**Mode:** essayistic

## Briefing

Friday morning. The next essay for the substack is due Sunday. I usually have a fragment by Friday — a sentence I overheard, a passage I underlined this week, a frustration with a department meeting that wants to become something more than complaint.

Here I think out loud about which fragment is alive enough to carry an essay, and what shape it wants. Not "help me write the essay." More like "talk to me until I find the door."

The essays are quieter than the book. They are the place where I let myself say things that won't survive the academic register. The mirror helps me trust the smaller voice without asking it to argue more than it's ready to.
