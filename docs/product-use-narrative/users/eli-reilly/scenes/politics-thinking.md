# Politics — thinking out loud

**Voice:** persona
**Journey:** finding-my-politics
**Personas:** organizer, writer

## Briefing

I read something. I heard something on a podcast. I had a conversation at a coalition meeting where I noticed I was about to repeat someone else's confident take and almost did and then didn't. Or I'm halfway through a book (still — it's still *Dawn of Everything*) and the chapter is doing something I want to think with.

Here I think out loud — not to perform a position, not to win an argument. To check the gap between my political vocabulary (confident) and my political understanding (still wide open). To notice when I'm about to claim something I haven't earned.

The mirror helps me stay with the question instead of arriving at the answer too fast.
