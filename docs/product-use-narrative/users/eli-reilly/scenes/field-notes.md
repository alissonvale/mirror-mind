# Field notes

**Voice:** persona
**Personas:** naturalist
**Length:** brief

## Briefing

Came back from a walk in the watershed, or from a coalition site visit, or from sitting still long enough to notice something — a bird I haven't seen here this season, a tree that's leafing out two weeks early, a creek bed running lower than it should be.

This is the place where I write the observation down before I lose it, and let it sit next to the other observations from this month and this season. The mirror doesn't try to make a claim from one note. It helps me notice when notes start to converge into something a coalition would want to know about.

Not science. Not poetry. Field notes — patient, specific, dated.
