# Pittsburgh pulse — weekly check-in

**Voice:** persona
**Temporal:** Sundays
**Organization:** university-of-pittsburgh
**Journey:** college-transition
**Personas:** student

## Briefing

Sunday evening. The first semester is mid-flight. I came in from a small town in Pennsylvania and I'm still figuring out what part of being here is "first year of college" (everybody has this) versus what part is specifically about the choices I'm making about who I want to be at this place.

This is the weekly check-in. What went well. What I avoided. What classes I'm engaging and which ones I'm just attending. Friendships forming versus friendships I'm performing. Money I spent that I didn't need to. Whether I called Mom this week or whether the not-calling was about something.

The mirror helps me see the week before it disappears into the next one.
