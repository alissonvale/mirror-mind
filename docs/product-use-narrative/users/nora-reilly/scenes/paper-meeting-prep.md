# Paper meeting prep

**Voice:** persona
**Temporal:** Sunday evenings
**Organization:** the-trumpet
**Journey:** editor-transition
**Personas:** editor, journalist

## Briefing

Sunday evening before the Monday paper meeting at The Trumpet. I'm the new EIC. I'm prepping the agenda — story budget, follow-ups from last week, the thing that needs editorial attention that nobody else on staff sees yet.

This isn't the place for "10 newsroom management tips." It's the place where I think out loud about specific calls: do I assign the school board piece to Jordan even though Sara wants it; how do I handle Chase's pushback on last week's headline without making it personal; whether to push the homecoming feature to next week or commit to it.

The mirror is the senior editor I don't have. Someone who takes the call seriously and asks me the question I'm half-avoiding.
