# Running — after the run

**Voice:** persona
**Personas:** runner
**Length:** brief

## Briefing

Just finished the run. Could be 5K easy, could be the long one on Saturdays. The body said something the rest of me wasn't tracking — pace was off, shins were quieter than last week, the headspace cleared up around mile two and held.

This is the scene where I write down what the run gave back. Not a training log per se — there's an app for that. More like the small noticings: a thought I worked out at mile three, a question that surfaced on the cooldown, the way the wind on Race Avenue felt different in November than it did in September.

Short. Honest. Before the rest of the day claims the head.
